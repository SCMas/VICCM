Nuestros Conferencistas

Laura Romero

Project leader

Laura is a founding partner, CO-CEO and Leader of the Development Agency, traveler, entrepreneur, lawyer and defender of nature. Laura sees tourism as a tool to generate a positive impact on rural communities and in the protection of biodiversity.

Carlos Pedraza

Monitoring Leader

Carlos is a Biologist, expert in remote sensing technology to provide data and science based monitoring solutions, Carlos has extensive experience in deforestation and conservation monitoring projects.

Camila Ariza

Project manager

Camila is a Project Manager at Awake, she is also the communication link with the hosts participating in the project. Camila is convinced that sustainable development and preservation of the heritage of the territories can be achived through experiential tourism.

Diego J. Lizcano

Acoustic Monitoring

Diego is a Biologist, an expert in ecology and biodiversity conservation, with experience in monitoring projects using various techniques and technologies applied to conservation.
